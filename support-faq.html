General

How do I book a service?

All bookings are made exclusively through the Esqyre mobile app to ensure a seamless and secure experience. Simply download the app from the App Store or Google Play, browse our collection, and book your desired asset in just a few taps.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express, directly within the app. Corporate clients have access to centralized invoicing and other payment options through the Corporate Suite.

For Corporate Clients

How do I sign up for a Corporate Account?

You can begin the application process by visiting our "Corporate Suite" page and clicking "Request Access". You will be guided through our digital onboarding flow. Once your application is reviewed and approved, you will get access to your corporate dashboard.

For Partners & Vendors

How do I list my assets on Esqyre?

To become a partner, please visit our "Partner With Us" page and click "Apply to List". You will be guided through our secure onboarding process to submit your company and asset details for verification.
